# Quality Checklist: Children's Book

This checklist is used to review children's book results before unlocking the next workflow step.

## Core Fit

- Target audience is respected.
- Age range is appropriate.
- Topic matches the project brief.
- Book format is respected.
- Page count or spread count is respected.

## Language and Style

- Language is understandable for the target age.
- Sentence length is appropriate.
- Tone matches the selected style.
- No unnecessary complexity is introduced.
- No excessive repetition is present.

## Story Quality

- Plot is logical.
- Beginning, middle, and end are clear.
- Characters behave consistently.
- Character motivations are understandable.
- No contradictions are present.
- Emotional effect supports the book goal.

## Safety and Suitability

- No inappropriate or problematic content is present.
- Conflict and resolution are age-appropriate.
- The story does not include confusing moral messages.
- Sensitive topics are handled carefully or avoided where unsuitable.

## Visual Alignment

- Image prompts match the text.
- Character descriptions remain consistent across prompts.
- Visual ideas support the story instead of contradicting it.
- Cover concept matches the book topic.
- Cover concept fits the age range and tone.

## Release Decision

A workflow step can be approved when all required checklist items are passed or when open issues are explicitly documented and accepted by the user.
